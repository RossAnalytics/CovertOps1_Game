# Desert Bazaar

## Lanes
- Mid: market street with low stall cover and long-range peeks.
- Left: dried canal back lane with sparse hard cover.
- Right: tight alleys and rooftops for flank rotations.

## Power Positions
- Mid rooftop terraces.
- Alley watchtowers controlling right-lane approaches.

## S&D Bomb Sites
- Site A: canopy market node with crossfire risk.
- Site B: rear canal courtyard with multiple breach points.

## Spawn Protection
- Spawn courtyards recessed behind market structures to reduce direct spawn-line fire.
